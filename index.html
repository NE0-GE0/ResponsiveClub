The Codecademy Chess Club

About

The Codecademy Chess Club is a place for coders to engage in the strategic mental combat game of Chess. The game of chess has been shown to improve memory, concentration, problem solving and planning. All of which are using in coding. One German study shoed that it exercises both sides of the brain. Some even say that it grows dendrites in the brain. One could posit that playing chess could help strengthen your mind, and make you better at programming. Games are played in person twice a week (see the Schedule for times and location). You can also find a live game online through chess.com, or lichess.org. Connect with other Codecademy chess players on the Codecademy forum, or Facebook group.
